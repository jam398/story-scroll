# Spec 08: Presentation Redesign

## Purpose

Define the redesign target for converting the current card-based scrollytelling page into an image-led presentation narrative.

## What Done Looks Like

- The page presents the League history as 8 to 12 clear visual beats.
- Slides feel like they enter and resolve with the scroll rather than simply stacking as article sections.
- Incoming backgrounds can rise from the bottom of the viewport and claim the scene before the text becomes the main focus layer.
- The copy is noticeably shorter and more memorable than the current implementation.
- The image strategy is deliberate, rights-safe, and strong enough to carry the story.
- The redesign remains grounded in the factual history source and static-export constraints.

## Constraints

- The redesign must preserve the existing factual timeline from Riot's founding through Arcane and the present-day legacy beat.
- The redesign must not exceed single-page scope.
- The redesign must avoid cloning the BSEAI presentation repo even when it borrows successful ideas such as sticky slide stages, image-led pacing, and scroll-driven reveal rhythm.
- The redesign must stay reviewable in bounded phases.
- Slide count may be reduced when adjacent beats merge cleanly and the narrative remains clear.
- Generic placeholder illustrations should be replaced where stronger approved backgrounds exist in the staged asset set.

## Non-Goals

- No conversion into a multi-route explainer with handout subpages.
- No dependence on proprietary image packs with unclear usage terms.
- No long-form encyclopedic treatment of League history.

## Acceptance Checks

- A reader can identify the opening premise, launch, ranked competition, esports rise, living-game model, culture shift, universe expansion, and legacy without reading long paragraphs.
- The redesign reduces text density relative to the current card-based page.
- The presentation uses imagery as a structural storytelling tool rather than as a small supporting decoration.
- The page can shift visual emphasis from background image to text focus during scroll progression without losing readability.
- The implementation plan can be broken into clear content, layout, asset, and validation phases.
