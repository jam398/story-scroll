League of Legends changed from one online game into a living platform, a global esport, and a larger entertainment universe.

---

Riot Games was founded in 2006 by Brandon Beck and Marc Merrill. The idea was not to ship a boxed game and walk away. It was to keep one competitive game alive with updates, balance changes, new champions, and ongoing player attention.

League's history is the story of a game that refused to stay frozen.

---

League of Legends launched on October 27, 2009. Riot framed it as a MOBA where two teams picked champions, fought across the Rift, and tried to destroy the enemy Nexus.

- Release date: October 27, 2009.
- Champions fill roles while lanes and jungle routes shape the match.
- The win condition stays clear: break the enemy Nexus first.

---

In 2010, Season One and ranked systems made improvement measurable. Ladders and draft-style selection gave players a reason to study, adapt, and keep climbing.

Ranked play turned League into a long-term competitive habit.

---

League's first major championship arrived in 2011 at DreamHack Summer in Jönköping, Sweden. Eight teams competed, and Fnatic won. That early event proved League could work as a spectator esport.

- 2011: Fnatic wins the first major championship at DreamHack Summer.
- Worlds keeps growing through regional leagues, spectacle, and annual scale.
- League becomes something global audiences watch together, not only play.

---

League survived because it kept changing. New champions, reworks, items, balance patches, and shifting metas kept the game fresh and sometimes frustrating.

That constant reinvention became the engine of League itself.

---

By the late 2010s, League was no longer only a game. Riot used music, cinematics, and event spectacle to turn League into a cultural stage.

K/DA's POP/STARS debut at Worlds in 2018 showed that champions could become performers and pop-culture icons.

---

At League's 10th anniversary in 2019, Riot made the next move clear. Teamfight Tactics and Wild Rift showed that Runeterra could stretch across formats and devices.

- Teamfight Tactics moved League champions into an autobattler.
- Wild Rift reworked League for mobile.
- Arcane premiered on Netflix in 2021, and 2024 activations connected that wider universe back into Riot's games.

---

League of Legends helped define the modern live-service competitive game. Its history is not just that a game got popular. It is a shift in how games can grow through updates, esports, culture, and media.

League began as a battle for the Nexus. It became a battle for what a game could be.
